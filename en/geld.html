What Is Money – and Why It Threatens Your Lifetime

by Alien Investor

Money Is Stored Lifetime

Every euro, every franc, every digital number in your account – it stands for something you sacrificed: your time, your energy, your nerves. Money is stored life effort. It is a kind of battery for your strength.

In this video I show you why money is more than printed paper – and how it is being stolen from you.

Money Has Taken Many Forms

Shells, beads, salt, gold, silver – humans have always searched for ways to store their exchange value. But not everything stood the test of time. Why? Because good money requires specific properties.

What Makes Good Money

  • Durability: It does not rot.
  • Divisibility: Even small units remain useful.
  • Portability: You can easily carry it with you.
  • Recognizability: It can be verified unambiguously.
  • Scarcity: It cannot be created arbitrarily.
  • Fungibility: Others recognize it as valuable.

Gold was not perfect – but it was good. That is why it held for millennia. Until a trick came along that changed everything.

The Trick: Fiat Money and the Grand Illusion

Today we live in the fiat age. States and central banks create money – without backing, without collateral, simply by pressing a button. It is not based on trust, but on coercion. And this money loses value every day. You work, they print.

Who Profits? Who Loses?

Those closest to the money source – central banks, major banks, large corporations – profit first. They receive fresh money before prices rise (Cantillon effect). The rest of the population picks up the tab. Inflation always hits the last in line first: you.

Conclusion: Money Is Energy – Protect It

Money is nothing other than stored lifetime. When the state devalues your money, it steals your life – silently, elegantly, systemically.

So: Protect your money. Protect your time. Protect yourself.

"Money is energy. And energy is life. Whoever controls your money controls you."
