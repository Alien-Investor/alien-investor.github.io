Request Analyses – Mini-FAQ

Plain talk, no fine print.

Here's the short, straight version of how request analyses work. No games, no promises, no finfluencer theater.

1. What is a request analysis?

A request analysis is a factual company analysis for a stock of your choice: business model, financials, opportunities, risks and context. No day trading, no hype, no price oracle.

2. How do I submit a request?

You can voluntarily support my work (Ko-fi, Bitcoin on-chain or Bitcoin Lightning). If you have a stock request, write it in the memo / message field.

Format example: Stock – Ticker
  • Apple – AAPL
  • Microsoft – MSFT
  • ASML – ASML

3. Why through donations?

A small barrier filters out spam and troll requests. Memos/messages are also quickly visible on Lightning. This keeps the signal quality high.

4. Is there a guarantee?

No. Analyses are done based on time and availability. There is no claim to completion or timing. Some topics are a better fit than others – I decide freely based on editorial interest.

5. Is this financial advice?

No. This is information and education. I do not give buy or sell recommendations, price targets or timing calls. You always make decisions yourself, on your own responsibility.

6. No memo field available?

If your wallet or provider does not support a message field, just send me a short email referencing the transaction and your request to:
kontakt@alien-investor.org
