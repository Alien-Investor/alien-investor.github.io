Sachwert-Tresor (Hard-Asset Vault)

Encrypted vault for Bitcoin, gold & silver

Your wealth is nobody's business. A local, encrypted vault for your Bitcoin, gold and silver holdings – fully offline. No cloud, no server, no price API reading along. Nothing leaves your device in plain text.

  • AES-256-GCM encryption, protected by your master password
  • Optional two-factor authentication (TOTP)
  • Precious metals as count × unit size – bars and coins tracked cleanly
  • Manual price entry: no data leaking to external price services
  • Cross-device sync via Syncthing – peer-to-peer, no middleman

Full hardening only in the app (v2.0): no INTERNET permission, FLAG_SECURE, no cloud backup – operating-system-level protection the web version cannot offer.

Android / GrapheneOS · open source (MIT) · also as a web version

Ausgaben-Tracker (Expense Tracker)

Minimal, fully offline expense tracker

Alien Fitness

Minimal, fully offline workout tracker

A training log that belongs to you. A personal workout tracker – training plans, live logging and progress, without a fitness platform recording your health data.

  • Training plans: strength (Push / Pull / Legs) and HIT (Tabata, Power)
  • Live session logging with automatic rest timer
  • Progress charts per exercise (max weight / reps over time)
  • Exercise library with images, muscle groups and equipment

Android / GrapheneOS · open source · requires login to the Alien Investor fitness backend
