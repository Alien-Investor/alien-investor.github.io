The Pillars

Bitcoin — Self-Custody or Nothing

Not your keys, not your coins. That is not advice — that is physics. ETFs, exchanges, custodians: all just promises. Bitcoin in self-custody is the only real Bitcoin ownership.

Real Ownership — DRS & Registered Shares

Buying stocks through a broker usually means holding a ledger entry, not shares. Real ownership means direct registration with the company (DRS) or registered shares. The difference becomes critical when brokers fail.
